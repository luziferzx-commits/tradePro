# GQOS: Quantitative Research Operating System
*Alpha is temporary. The Pipeline is permanent.*

GQOS is an institutional-grade Quantitative Research Operating System designed to outlive any individual strategy. It maximizes the organization's ability to continuously discover, validate, deploy, monitor, retire, and replace Alpha through a scientifically rigorous, evidence-driven research pipeline.

## Architecture
GQOS is built on a highly decoupled event-driven architecture.
- **Core Engine**: Deterministic `EventBus`, `Command` pattern, and plugin registry.
- **Execution**: Multi-Broker Adapter factory with strict rate-limiting and shielding.
- **Risk & Accounting**: Real-time `ExposureEngine`, `KillSwitches`, and `Ledger`.
- **Portfolio**: `Hierarchical Risk Parity (HRP)` and dynamic capital staging.
- **Research**: `AlphaFactory`, `ExperimentTracker`, and `FailureAnalytics`.

## Research Workflow
The lifecycle of Alpha within GQOS:
1. **Generation**: `IAlphaModel` permutations generated by the Factory.
2. **Validation**: Filtered strictly by Probability of Backtest Overfitting (PBO) and Superior Predictive Ability (SPA).
3. **Shadow Trading**: Promoted to `ShadowRouter` for 90 days of $0.00 execution to track true slippage and decay.
4. **Live Execution**: Capital is staged (Micro $\rightarrow$ Small $\rightarrow$ Normal $\rightarrow$ Institutional).
5. **Retirement**: Demoted to Archive if `AlphaHealthScore` violations occur.
